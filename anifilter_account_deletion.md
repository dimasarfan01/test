# Account & Data Deletion — AniFilter AI Photo Editor

**App:** AniFilter AI Photo Editor
**Developer:** Shura Tech Global
**Contact:** dimasarfanasyuro123@gmail.com
**Last Updated: June 9, 2026**

AniFilter AI Photo Editor lets you permanently delete your account and the
data associated with it. You can do this yourself inside the app, or by
emailing us. This page explains both options and what happens to your data.

---

## Option 1 — Delete your account in the app (recommended)

1. Open **AniFilter AI Photo Editor** and make sure you are signed in.
2. Go to the **Settings** tab (bottom-right).
3. Scroll to the **Account** section.
4. Tap **Delete Account**.
5. Confirm by tapping **Delete** in the dialog.

Your account and associated data are deleted **immediately**, and you are
signed out. This action cannot be undone.

---

## Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, email us and we will delete your
account for you:

- **Email:** dimasarfanasyuro123@gmail.com
- **Subject:** Account Deletion Request
- **Include:** the email address you used to sign in (Google account), so we
  can locate and verify your account.

We process these requests within **30 days** and email you to confirm once
your account has been deleted.

---

## What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove all of the following
from our systems:

- Your **account profile** — name, email address, and profile picture
  obtained from Google Sign-In.
- Your **token balance** and your full **token transaction history**
  (purchases, bonuses, refunds, and ad-reward credits).
- Your **generation history** (records of the anime styles you generated).
- Any **purchase verification** and **rewarded-ad** records linked to your
  account.

## What data is retained (and for how long)

- We **do not** store your uploaded photos or the generated result images on
  our servers — they are processed only to create your image and are not
  retained, so there is nothing to delete on our side.
- We may retain limited records **only where required by law** (for example,
  transaction or tax records required for completed purchases). Where this
  applies, the data is kept solely for legal compliance and is deleted once
  the required retention period ends.
- **Purchases are non-refundable.** Deleting your account does not refund any
  past token purchases, and any remaining token balance is forfeited.
